## [SpotiPy Wrapped:](https://github.com/TheSupremeToast/SpotiPy-Wrapped)

* Written in Python and R
* Utilizes the Spotify API, ggplot, dplyr, Tidyverse, Matplotlib, Pandas, and Jupyter
